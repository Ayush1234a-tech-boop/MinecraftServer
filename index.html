Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Lifesteal server

What is a Lifesteal SMP?

A Lifesteal SMP is a Minecraft survival server where players can steal hearts (health) from each other. When you kill another player, you gain one of their hearts, increasing your maximum health. If you die, you lose a heart. This creates intense PvP scenarios where every battle matters.

How do I join the server?

Simply add our server IP soulsmp4.minefort.com:25565 to your Minecraft server list and connect. You'll need Minecraft Java Edition or Minecraft Bedrock Edition with any version to join. No whitelist is required - just join and start playing!

Is there a Discord server?

Yes! We have an active Discord community where players coordinate, report issues, and participate in events. You can join our Discord by clicking the "Discord" button in the navigation or footer of this website.

What happens if I lose all my hearts?

If you reach zero hearts, you'll be temporarily banned from the server. However, you can earn your way back by participating in special events or waiting for the next season reset. We also occasionally run "revival" events where players can regain access.

Are there any pay-to-win features?

No! Soul SMP is completely free to play with no pay-to-win elements.

How often does the server reset?

We run seasonal resets approximately every 3-4 weeks. This keeps the gameplay fresh and gives new players a chance to compete. Major updates may also prompt a reset. All players are notified well in advance of any reset.
